Inquiries

Do you work internationally?

Yes. While we are based in Kyoto and Berlin, we accept commissions globally. We specialize in adapting our brutalist-organic philosophy to varied climates and topographies.

What is your sustainability approach?

We prioritize passive design principles, locally sourced materials, and renewable energy integration. Our goal is net-zero carbon impact for every structure we build.

Do you handle interior design?

Absolutely. We believe the interior experience is inseparable from the architectural form. Our team provides holistic design services including custom furniture and lighting.
